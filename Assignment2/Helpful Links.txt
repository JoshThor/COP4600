Commands:

(Build kernal module)
make

(Install device)
sudo insmod charDev.ko

(Create device file)
sudo mknod /dev/charDev c 247 0
sudo chmod a+r+w /dev/charDev

(Write to device)
 echo “hello” >/dev/charDev

(Read from device)
cat /dev/charDev
